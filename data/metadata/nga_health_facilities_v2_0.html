Purpose: The GRID3 NGA - Health Facilities v2.0 dataset is a non-exhaustive, non-validated geographic representation of health facility points in Nigeria. The dataset incorporates recent updates from both the Nigeria Health Facility Registry (HFR) and GRID3. This dataset is considered operational.
